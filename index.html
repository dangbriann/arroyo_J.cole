Who is J.Cole

J. Cole is an American rapper,songwriter, and record producer. J.Cole signed to Jay Z's Roc Nation in 2009. He has also made his own record label called Dreamville. J. Cole as had great success over the years, that's a result of him spening long hours working in the studio, grinding hard on his mixtape. His first mixtape debut in 2007. His father was in the army and left his mother then his mom became addicted to crack under the influence of her new boyfriend. Since signing to Roc Nation he has went on world wide tours, sold huge amounts of records, he produced for Kendrick Lamar, and even caught the attention of Barack Obama. J. Cole was born in Germany and then moved to North Carolina. He was born on January 28 1985, at a U.S. Army base in Frankfurt.
